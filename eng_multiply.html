Braille Symbol: Multiply (×)

Composition of Braille 'Multiply'

Braille 'Multiply' symbol is formed by **dots 2, 3, 5, 6 (middle left, bottom left, middle right, bottom right)** in the 6-dot Braille system.

This symbol is represented in a single cell.

Reading Order

For the Braille 'Multiply' symbol, check the middle left, bottom left, middle right, and bottom right dots.

Check the presence of dots from top to bottom, and from left to right.

Other Explanations

The multiply symbol is used in mathematics to denote the multiplication operation.

It is used to express a multiplicative relationship between numbers.
