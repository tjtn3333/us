Braille Consonant: H

Composition of Braille 'H'

Braille 'H' is formed by **dots 1, 2, 5** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'H', identify the top left, middle left, and middle right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅎ' (H) is a consonant used in braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 2, 5** based on the 6-dot braille system.
