Braille Symbol: Divide (รท)

Composition of Braille 'Divide'

Braille 'Divide' symbol is formed by **dots 3, 5, 6 (bottom left, middle right, bottom right)** in the 6-dot Braille system.

This symbol is represented across two cells.

Reading Order

For the Braille 'Divide' symbol, check the bottom left dot in the first cell, and the middle right and bottom right dots in the second cell.

Check the presence of dots from top to bottom, and from left to right.

Other Explanations

The divide symbol is used in mathematics to denote the division operation.

It is used to express a division relationship between numbers.
