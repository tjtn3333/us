Braille Consonant: D

Composition of Braille 'D'

Braille 'D' is formed by **dots 1, 4** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'D', identify the top left and top right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㄷ' (D) is an essential consonant for learning braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 4** based on the 6-dot braille system.
