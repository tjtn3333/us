Braille Consonant: B

Composition of Braille 'B'

Braille 'B' is formed by **dots 1, 2, 5** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'B', identify the top left, middle left, and middle right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅂ' (B) is one of the basic consonants for learning braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 2, 5** based on the 6-dot braille system.
