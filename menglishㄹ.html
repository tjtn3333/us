Braille Consonant: R/L

Composition of Braille 'R/L'

Braille 'R/L' is formed by **dots 1, 2, 4** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'R/L', identify the top left, middle left, and top right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㄹ' (R/L) is an important consonant for learning braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 2, 4** based on the 6-dot braille system.
