Braille Consonant: N

Composition of Braille 'N'

Braille 'N' is formed by **dots 1, 2** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'N', start from the top left and identify the raised dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㄴ' (N) is one of the basic consonants for learning braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 2** based on the 6-dot braille system.
