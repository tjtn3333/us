Braille Consonant: S

Composition of Braille 'S'

Braille 'S' is formed by **dots 1, 2, 4, 5** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'S', identify the top left, middle left, top right, and middle right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅅ' (S) is a frequently used consonant in braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 2, 4, 5** based on the 6-dot braille system.
