Braille Consonant: K

Composition of Braille 'K'

Braille 'K' is formed by **dots 1, 4, 6** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'K', identify the top left, top right, and bottom right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅋ' (K) is a consonant used in braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 1, 4, 6** based on the 6-dot braille system.
