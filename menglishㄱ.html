Braille Consonant: G/K

Composition of Braille 'G/K'

Braille 'G/K' is formed by **dot 1** of the 6 dots. It is one of the most basic braille characters, consisting of only one dot in a cell.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'G/K', start from the top left and identify the raised dot.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㄱ' (G/K) is a fundamental starting point for learning braille and is used to form various words and sentences. This braille character is formed by **dot 1** based on the 6-dot braille system.
