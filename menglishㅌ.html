Braille Consonant: T

Composition of Braille 'T'

Braille 'T' is formed by **dots 2, 3, 4, 5** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'T', identify the middle left, bottom left, top right, and middle right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅌ' (T) is a consonant used in braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 2, 3, 4, 5** based on the 6-dot braille system.
