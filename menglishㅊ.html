Braille Consonant: CH

Composition of Braille 'CH'

Braille 'CH' is formed by **dots 2, 4, 6** of the 6 dots.

Reading Order

To read Braille 'CH', identify the middle left, top right, and bottom right dots.

Other Explanations

The Korean consonant 'ㅊ' (CH) is an important consonant for learning braille. This braille character is formed by **dots 2, 4, 6** based on the 6-dot braille system.
